IN 2021, NSB EARNS THE GREATEST PROFIT OF RS. 22 BILLION


National Savings Bank (NSB) Chairperson Keshila Jayawardena stated that the firm earned a net profit of Rs.22 billion in 2021, the highest in the bank's history. She stated that this occurred as a result of her participation in activities such as utilizing organic fertilizer to boost the harvest of the rich and sumptuous field known as the NSB. She made this remark during the bank's Golden Jubilee (50th Anniversary) celebrations yesterday (16) morning at the NSB Headquarters in Kollupitiya, Colombo.

Yesterday marked the 50th anniversary of the National Savings Bank (NSB), which educated the people of our country about thrift and the practice of saving cent by cent and rupee by rupee.pomp and circumstance at its peak yesterday as well as at 261 branches nationwide.

However, she stated that it was feasible to achieve the greatest profit after tax, Rs. 22 billion, in the NSB's history in 2021. In the preceding year, the net profit was Rs. 10.5 billion.
