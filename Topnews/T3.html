PAt the UAE SWAT Challenge 2022, the STF SWAT squad received excellent recognition.


At the UAE SWAT Challenge 2022, conducted by Dubai Police, Major General Abdullah Ali Al Ghaithi, Director of Organizations Protective Security and Emergency, commended the SWAT units of the Sri Lanka Police Special Task Forces.
