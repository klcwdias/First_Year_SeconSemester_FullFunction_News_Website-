World Athletics Indoor Championships 2022: Schedule and BBC coverage times


From Friday to Sunday, the World Athletics Indoor Championships will be held in Belgrade, Serbia, and the action will be broadcast live on BBC TV and radio.

Katarina Johnson-Thompson, the incumbent pentathlon champion, and Andrew Pozzi, the men's 60m hurdles champion, will be hoping to defend their championships.

Long jumper Lorraine Ugen and sprinter Daryll Neita, as well as 800m runners Keely Hodgkinson and Elliot Giles, have high aspirations.

Marcell Jacobs, the Italian 100m Olympic winner, competes against Christian Coleman, the defending indoor champion, in the men's 60m.

Another standout race is the women's 400m, which pits twice Olympic winner Shaunae Miller-Uibo of the Bahamas against rising star Femke Bol of the Netherlands.

Mondo Duplantis of Sweden, meantime, will attempt to break his world record for the second time this month.

Former Olympic heptathlon champion Dame Jessica Ennis-Hill, world indoor 60m champion Richard Kilty, and marathon world record holder Paula Radcliffe will be joined by TV presenters Jeanette Kwakye and Gabby Logan on Friday, world indoor 60m champion Richard Kilty on Saturday, and marathon world record holder Paula Radcliffe on Sunday.

Alison Curbishley will be joined by Katherine Merry, a former 400m runner, on BBC radio for live coverage of the evening sessions on Saturday and Sunday.
